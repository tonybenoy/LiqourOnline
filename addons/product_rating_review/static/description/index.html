Product Rating

Video Tutorial

Odoo allows the users to provide ratings and reviews to every product. Moreover, other users can also see the reviews and ratings provided by particular user for the product. Now, the challenge was that administrator could not view what all comments and how many ratings were received by any product from backend.

As a solution to this, Aktiv Software prepared this module which helps the admin to review the product rating average and comments posted by different users from backend. Hence, the admin/sales manager can easily identify the product reviews and ratings and check whether any improvements are required for that particular product.

Prerequisites before Installation

No prerequisites needed.

Configurations

Enable this future from: Website -> Shop -> Product -> Customize ->Discussion and Rating

On the basis of ratings given by customers, an average rating will be generated for particular product and will be displayed in the backend.

As mentioned above, a customer review tab will be added under the product details which will highlight different reviews provided by customers.
